About Us

Cafe Chai Coffee

Located in the heart of South Goa, surrounded by lush greenery and forest-inspired ambiance. Whether you are planning a relaxed morning, a date night, or an evening out with friends — we celebrate the spirit of the coast with flavours from Continental, Asian, and Indian cuisines. From a four-chair nook to an 80-seater café, bar, and beyond, Cafe Chai Coffee has evolved into a vibrant destination where every guest feels like family.

Our story

In 2020, when the pandemic struck, Sneha Rano, an architect and urban designer based in Hyderabad, was on maternity leave with a newborn. Instead of waiting for the world to return to normal, she found purpose in helping others — delivering freshly cooked meals to frontline workers, migrant labourers, and families in need.

Her parents, Colonel Dr. Satnam Singh Bajwa and Surinder Kaur Bajwa, encouraged her to turn that compassion into something sustainable. From a four-chair nook to a 60-seater restaurant and bar, Café Chai Coffee has grown into a vibrant dining destination. Alongside it thrives BAOWICH, Sneha's Asian-inspired brand — together offering an artisanal banquet space for 40–100 guests.

At CCC, every detail — from design to dishes — is inspired by nature and crafted with love. Whether you are meeting friends, enjoying a family lunch, or a night of live music, we bring you flavours and moments worth remembering. We welcome you to Café Chai Coffee & Baowich — built on warmth, creativity, and community.

— Sneha Rano Bhandare
